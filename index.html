My list

This is the list of my favorite movies:

  1. Interestellar

    2. Interstellar is a science fiction film directed by Christopher Nolan. The story follows a group of astronauts who travel through a wormhole in search of a new home for humanity, facing scientific and emotional challenges in space.

  2. Star Wars

    3. Star Wars is an epic space opera that follows the battle between the Jedi and the Sith, featuring iconic characters, intergalactic adventures, and the struggle between good and evil in a galaxy far, far away.

  3. Infinity War

    4. Avengers: Infinity War is a high-stakes Marvel epic where the heroes race to stop Thanos from collecting the Infinity Stones and wiping out half the universe. Its my favorite because the ensemble action, shocking twists, and emotional stakes make every rewatch intense and unforgettable.

  4. Taxi Driver

    5. Taxi Driver is a gritty character study of Travis Bickle, a lonely NYC cabbie whose isolation and disillusionment spiral into violence in a decaying New York City.
    Its my favorite because its haunting mood, raw performances, and Scorseses precise craft linger long after the credits.

  5. How to Train Your Dragon

    6. How to Train Your Dragon is an animated adventure about Hiccup, a young Viking who befriends a dragon and changes the way his village sees these creatures.
    Its my favorite because its heartfelt, visually stunning, and shows the power of friendship and understanding.
